Wouter M. Kouw

Post-doctoral Fellow

University of Copenhagen


I am a postdoc at the Image Analysis, Computational Modeling & Geometry Section at the University of Copenhagen. My work focuses on domain-adaptive machine learning, but I am generally interested in statistical models, algorithms and designing systems that produce intelligent behaviour.

My position is funded through the Niels Stensen Fellowship.
